Welcome

We are a small hobby breeder located on two acres in beautiful rural Greenwood Village CO. Our goal is to raise outstanding Australian Shepherds that are not only beautiful but have good structure, superb temperaments, excellent health and make wonderful companions. Our dogs are our family and treasured companions first, competition dogs second and we occasionally breed when we are looking to keep something for ourselves. We train and compete in conformation, obedience, rally, nose work and agility. When we are not training for competition you will often find us with the dogs hiking in the foothills, walking or biking along the Highline Canal or in the winter skijoring or snowshoeing in the mountains.
