Shimmer

CH Absoloot Shine Bright @ Highline

About Shimmer

When a good friend asked me to help her find a nice show prospect I didn’t hesitate to recommend she look at my friend Keli Gaunt’s litter. I had seen pictures of the puppies and knew immediately it was an exceptional litter with a wonderful pedigree. I put Joan in touch with Keli and after evaluations were done she was offered a lovely black Tri female. Joan asked me if I would be interested in buying her together on a co-own and I didn’t hesitate to say yes. Shimmer arrived at 10 weeks of age and both Joan and I were thrilled with her soundness, type, and confident personality. Shimmer has proven to be an outstanding show dog, garnering some very competitive wins in her short career.

Highlights:

2018 ASCA Nationals Pre-Show 2nd place 2-4 months under BJ Sharon Sparks. She was entered into two 4-6 Beginner Puppy AKC shows in which she took BOB and Group 1. At 6 months she made her ASCA debut and came home with a 4 point Major under SBJ Annette Cyboron. A few months later she picked up 3 point and 4 point ASCA majors.

2019 ASCA Nationals 15-18 month class (Handler Kat Wallis):

  • 1st Place Delta ASC pre-show - SBJ Clarissa Shanks
  • Winners Bitch Gold Country ASC - SBJ Dianna Williams (To Finish)
  • BOS Nationals Sweepstakes - SBJ Heidi Mobley
  • 1st Place Nationals - SBJ Laura Shivers

AKC (Handler Kat Wallis):

  • RWB to a Major
  • WB/BOS 4 points
  • WB/BOW 4 points

She will be campaigned in ASCA 2020/21 for finals to be held in Texas in the fall of 2021.

Shimmer is currently in training for agility and rally where she shows great promise and will soon start stock which she has already proven to have natural talent to for.

Shimmer will be bred in the spring of 2021. Check our litter page closer to that time for details.
