About Crush

Crush came to us from our friends at Sonkist Australian Shepherds in California. He is out of HOF BISS/BIS CH Carolina Calais On The Fly, (number 1 ASCA intact 2008/2009) and ASCA/AKC CH Calais Carolina Call Me Classic, DNA-VP Crush did not make it as a show dog and was neutered at 15 months but he was a very much loved member of the family who soon found his calling as a service dog for my son Ross who has type 1 diabetes. Crush is a very smart, empathetic dog who very quickly became an expert at detecting low blood sugar. On more than one occasion he has prevented Ross from going into a diabetic coma and has even alerted complete strangers to low blood sugar.
