AKC GCH/ASCA CH Highline’s To Sir With Love (LULU) has been bred and confirmed pregnant to AKC/ASCA CH Calais Carolina Your Place Or Mine (CHANDLER)

We expect exceptionally happy, confident, biddable and beautiful puppies from this cross. Blues and blacks possible.

As it looks like a large litter we may have a couple of companion puppies available. If you are interested in a puppy please complete our puppy questionnaire (Enter link)or contact us for more details on this litter.

Puppies are sold on spay/neuter contracts only. Our puppies are priced at $2000 and come with eye exams with a licensed ophthalmologist, health check, first vaccinations and microchips. They have started crate training, clicker training, leash training and have been exposed to many different social environments.

Most people know not to buy from pet stores as doing so supports puppy mills. But how do you find a reputable breeder and what is the difference between a hobby breeder and a backyard breeder? Read this excellent comparison by Victoria Rose.

A couple of things I would like to add. A reputable breeder never charges more for a certain coat or eye color. They don’t charge more for a female over a male. They also would never have a buy it now option on their website. If you are considering buying from a breeder that does any of the above then you are looking at buying from a backyard breeder.
