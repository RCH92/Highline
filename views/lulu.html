About Lulu

Lulu is my heart dog and I couldn’t imagine life without this sassy, biddable and brilliant girl who lives life to the fullest and excels at what ever I ask of her. Lulu followed in her mother Allie’s footsteps with an extremely successful conformation career. She took her first four and five point ASCA majors out of the 9-12 class going BOW both times and a BOS over a nice group of specials owner handled. She won the huge 9-12 puppy class three times at nationals and thrilled us by taking RWB at one of the pre-shows beautifully handled by good friend kristina Churchill. She finished both AKC and ASCA championships with ease by going BOB in both venues over multiple lovely specials. Not only is lulu beautifully put together but she is the most talented performance dog I’ve ever had the good fortune to own. Whether it’s rally, obedience, herding, barn hunt or agility she approaches each discipline with drive, focus and an abundance of natural talent. Lulu has been tested on livestock and not surprisingly she shows a ton of natural aptitude, she will start her herding career once she’s had her first litter.

Lulu out did herself at the ASCA 2019 nationals, even though it was her first time entered in both excellent and masters rally she took High Combined over some amazing competition in a very stressful trial environment.

The sky’s the limit for this girl, we have some lofty goals in our future and I have no doubt that we will achieve them.
